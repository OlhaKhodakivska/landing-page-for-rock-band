Our Story

«Thunder & Grit» was born from a desire to create music that is felt by the heart. We started our journey in a small studio in the city center, where every chord and every word was filled with emotions and experiences. This was the place where our first songs were born, and where we learned to play together as a single organism.

Over the years, we have performed on dozens of stages, from small clubs to major festivals. Our music is a blend of traditional rock with modern elements, making it unique and recognizable. We are not afraid to experiment with sound, adding electronic elements or folk instruments.

Every performance is not just a concert, but a true event where we share our energy with the audience and create a unique atmosphere. We believe that music has the power to unite people, build communities, and inspire change.

Our collective consists of experienced musicians, each bringing their unique style and vision. Max creates incredible guitar riffs, Elena enchants with her vocals, and Tyler keeps a rhythm that makes hearts beat in unison with the music.
